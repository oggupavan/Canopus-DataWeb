ML is the study of computer algorithms that improve themselves over time through experience .

ML Online Training Course

Machine learning is the process of computers figuring out how to accomplish things without being specifically taught to do so.It entails computers learning from data in order to do certain jobs. It is feasible to write algorithms informing the machine how to perform all steps required to solve the problem at hand for basic jobs entrusted to computers; no learning is required on the computer's side.

It might be difficult for a human to manually construct the algorithms required for increasingly sophisticated jobs. In practise, assisting the computer in developing its own algorithm rather than having human programmers define each required step can prove to be more successful.
